Who am I...
I am Gustavo Meneses, born and raised in Curitiba, Brazil. Decided to find out more about the world and now I am a Full stack developer in the UK! My love from computers and machines goes back to since I was a little boy and started playing around with them, fixing problems so I could play my own games.
I graduated from a bootcamp with LeWagon where I was able to learn how to set up webpages with Ruby on Rails, in the last few days I am learning how to make a page seem fast and responsive with Hotwired. Of course it goes without saying that I can talk to a database using SQL, build APIs and get them to respond to me with their JSONs. My passion is the back-end because it sounds more like computer dark magics.
